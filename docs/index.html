TABVERSE

An Opinionated Way of Managing Tabs

Install (Coming Soon)Download Beta(.crx)

With Tabverse you can bravely closing that many tabs!

Tabverse helps you to best managing many chrome tabs opened for doing a project.

Group your tabs and manage them with Tabverse

Group your tabs in one window and create Tabverse to manage them. One window, one Tabverse, and one workspace, focusing on one topic you are working.

You focus on working and let Tabverse focus on remembering your workspace

Save your Tabverse, and it will remember your tabs, todos, notes, and bookmarks automatically. Finishing your job? Feel free to close them as it is easy for find back with Tabverse.

You are master of your data. No login, no subscription, just convenience.

Tabverse saves all your data inside your local Chrome storage. You do not need an account to enjoy it!

Free and open source!

You can use it free by installing from Chrome web store. We also open the source code through our github repo. Not satisfy on something? Contribute it or, fork it!
